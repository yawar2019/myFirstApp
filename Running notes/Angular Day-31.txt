life cycle of hooks

1) constructor is called first and used for performing dependecy Injection
or anything you want to execute before any hooks life cycle method is called

2)ngOnInit
is going to be called after Constructor and best usage is to initialize Values

3)ngOnchanges Method
Called whenever your @Input() find Changes 
its parameter is to check the current ,previous,firstChanged detail
need to be define inside child component

4)ngDestroy

its called wheneever you navigate from one Component to Another
or leave anyother Component ngDestroy method is Called

5)ngDoCheck

this method is called whenever any changes it detect inside the Variable,property,arrays

6)ngAfterContentInit:
is called whenever you are going to load Data
it is also called whenever you are going to pass data to ng-content directives

7)ngAfterContentChecked

it is called to check data

8)ngAfterViewInit:

called to initialize Components

9) ngAfterChecked

called when your data is loaded or fully loaded
